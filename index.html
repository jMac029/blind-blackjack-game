BLIND BLACK JACK

HOW TO PLAY

A random number is assigned to each round of play

There are four cards below. Click each card to add a specific amount of points to your total score.

Win the round by matching your total score to the random number without going over.

The value of each card is hidden and is reassigned in each round of play.

Good Luck! and may the odds forever be in your favor!
